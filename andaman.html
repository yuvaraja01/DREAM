Detailed Itinerary

PORT BLAIR Day 1: Arrival + Corbyn’s Cove + Cellular Jail

  • Arrival at Veer Savarkar Airport (IXZ)
  • Transfer to hotel & freshen up
  • Visit Corbyn’s Cove Beach
  • Cellular Jail National Memorial
  • Evening: Light & Sound Show
  • Overnight: Port Blair

HAVELOCK Day 2: Port Blair → Havelock + Radhanagar Beach

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Private Ferry to Havelock Island
  • Transfer to hotel
  • Afternoon: Radhanagar Beach (Asia’s Best Beach)
  • Sunset views
  • Overnight: Havelock

Day 3: Elephant Beach (Snorkeling)

  • Breakfast
  • Speed boat to Elephant Beach
  • Complimentary Snorkeling (by Boat Association)
  • Coral viewing & water activities
  • Overnight: Havelock

NEIL Day 4: Havelock → Neil Island

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Ferry to Neil Island
  • Visit Bharatpur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach
  • Natural Coral Bridge
  • Overnight: Neil

Day 5: Neil → Port Blair

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Ferry back to Port Blair
  • Visit Sagarika Emporium for souvenirs
  • Free time for shopping
  • Overnight: Port Blair

Day 6: Departure

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Drop to Port Blair Airport
  • *** TOUR ENDS WITH MEMORIES OF PARADISE ***
