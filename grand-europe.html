Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive at Paris - the City of Romance, Lights and Glamour.

Welcome! Today, we embark on our journey to Paris, a city celebrated for its high fashion, world-famous museums, awe-inspiring landmarks, and captivating cabarets. Upon arrival, you’ll be warmly welcomed by our Travel Professional, who will guide you to your hotel and provide assistance with the check-in process.
Overnight in Paris. (Dinner)

Day 2: Guided city tour of Paris. Visit Eiffel Tower 3rd Level. Palace of Versailles.

Guided city tour including Place Vendôme, Opéra Garnier, Musée d’Orsay, Champs Elysées, Arc de Triomphe, and more. Ascend to the 3rd Level of the Eiffel Tower for panoramic views. Visit the Palace of Versailles — a UNESCO masterpiece with 15 million annual visitors.

Note: If 3rd level is closed, 2nd level will be provided.
Overnight in Paris. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 3: Disneyland® Paris + Seine Cruise + Paris by Night

Full day at Disneyland® Paris — choose Disney Parks or Walt Disney Studios. Later, enjoy a romantic Seine River Cruise and a magical Paris by Night Illumination Tour featuring Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame, Louvre Pyramid, and more.

Itinerary subject to change for logistics. All attractions covered.
Overnight in Paris. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 4: Brussels + Keukenhof (till May 12) or Madurodam (from May 13)

Visit Brussels: Grand Place, Manneken Pis, Town Hall. Then to Netherlands: Keukenhof Gardens (till May 12) or Madurodam Miniature Park (from May 13).
Overnight in Netherlands. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 5: Amsterdam Canal Cruise. Drive to Germany

Glass-topped boat cruise in Amsterdam. Drive to Frankfurt area.
Overnight in Germany. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 6: Mercedes Benz Museum + Black Forest + Rhine Falls

Visit Mercedes Benz Museum (or Rhine Falls boat ride on Monday). Drive to Black Forest: cuckoo clock demo. See Rhine Falls in Schaffhausen.
Overnight in Central Switzerland. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 7: Interlaken Horse Carriage + Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe

Horse-drawn carriage ride in Interlaken. Excursion to Jungfraujoch: Ice Palace, Sphinx Observatory, Aletsch Glacier.
Overnight in Central Switzerland. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 8: Mt. Titlis with Rotair + Lucerne + Swiss Chocolate Adventure

Ride the Rotair to Mt. Titlis (3,020m). Walk on Cliff Walk. Orientation tour of Lucerne: Lion Monument, Kapellbrücke. Visit Swiss Chocolate Adventure.
Overnight in Central Switzerland. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 9: Vaduz (Liechtenstein) Train Ride + Swarovski + Innsbruck

Mini train ride in Vaduz. Visit Swarovski Crystal Museum. Orientation tour of Innsbruck: Golden Roof, Maria Theresien Strasse.
Overnight in Innsbruck/Seefeld. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 10: Venice + Gondola Ride

Private boat to St. Mark’s Square. See Basilica, Bell Tower, Bridge of Sighs. Enjoy a romantic Gondola Ride.
Overnight in Padova/Bologna. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 11: Florence Guided Tour + Leaning Tower of Pisa

Guided walking tour: Duomo, Campanile, Baptistery, Piazza Della Signoria, Ponte Vecchio, Piazzale Michelangelo. Photo-stop at Leaning Tower of Pisa.
Overnight in Tuscany. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 12: Rome + Vatican City

Guided tour: Vatican Museum, Sistine Chapel, St. Peter’s Basilica. See Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, Victor Emmanuel Monument, Roman Forum.
Overnight in Rome. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Day 13: Fly Back Home

Bid farewell with lifelong memories. Transfer to airport.
(Breakfast)
