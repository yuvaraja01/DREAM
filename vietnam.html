Detailed Itinerary

HALONG Day 1 (01 Mar 2025): HANOI ARRIVAL – HALONG – OVERNIGHT CRUISE {L/D}

Arrival at Noi Bai Airport. Transfer to Halong Bay (2.5 hrs). Board cruise, welcome drink, check-in cabin. Lunch onboard. Afternoon activities (kayaking, cave visit). Dinner & overnight on cruise.

BOAT (3★): HAL5 LA REGINA CLASSIC
BOAT (4★): HAL4 VERDURE LOTUS CRUISE 16 CABIN
BOAT (5★): HAL5 AMBASSADOR CRUISE HALONG

Day 2 (02 Mar 2025): HALONG BAY – HANOI CITY HALF DAY {G/B/Br}

Morning cruise. Brunch. Disembark. Transfer to Hanoi. Visit Ho Chi Minh Complex, Mausoleum, Tran Quoc Pagoda, Hoan Kiem Lake, Old Quarter walk.

HOTEL (3★): HAN3 MOON VIEW HOTEL
HOTEL (4★): HAN4 CHALCEDONY HOTEL HANOI
HOTEL (5★): HAN5 GRAND VISTA HOTEL

Day 3 (03 Mar 2025): HANOI - HOA LU - TAM COC FULL DAY {G/B}

Drive to Ninh Binh. Visit Hoa Lu ancient capital, Dinh & Le temples. Bich Dong Pagoda. Sampan ride through Tam Coc (“Inland Halong”). Return to Hanoi.

Same hotels as Day 2

DA NANG Day 4 (04 Mar 2025): HANOI → DA NANG {G/B}

Flight to Da Nang. Transfer to hotel. Half-day city tour: Marble Mountains, stone village, Huyen Khong Cave, Han Market.

HOTEL (3★): DAD3 SENORITA BOUTIQUE HOTEL
HOTEL (4★): DAD4 GRAND GOLD DA NANG
HOTEL (5★): DAD5 DLG HOTEL DA NANG

Day 5 (05 Mar 2025): DA NANG - BANA HILL FULL DAY {G/B}

Cable car to Ba Na Hills. Visit Golden Bridge, Jardin D’Amour, Linh Ung Pagoda, Fantasy Park. King Peak view. Return to Da Nang.

Same hotels as Day 4

Day 6 (06 Mar 2025): DA NANG DEPARTURE {B}

Free time. Transfer to airport. End of tour.
