Detailed Itinerary

PARIS Day 1: Arrive Paris

Arrival in Paris. Meet tour manager. Transfer to hotel. Relax.

Overnight: B&B / Millennium / Hilton or similar – Paris (Dinner)

Day 2: Paris City Tour + Eiffel 3rd Level + Versailles + Seine Cruise

Guided city tour: Place Vendôme, Opéra, Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe. Eiffel Tower 3rd Level. Palace of Versailles. Romantic Seine Cruise.

Overnight: Same hotel – Paris (B, L, D)

Day 3: Disneyland Paris (Choice of Park)

Full day at Disneyland® Paris – Disney Park or Walt Disney Studios®.

Overnight: Same hotel – Paris (B, Packed L, D)

SWITZERLAND Day 4: Drive to Switzerland

Scenic drive through Alps. Arrive Central Switzerland.

Overnight: Radisson / Ramada / Movenpick or similar (B, L, D)

Day 5: Mt. Titlis + Rotair + Cliff Walk + Lucerne + Lake Cruise

Mt. Titlis with Rotair cable car. Cliff Walk. Orientation tour of Lucerne. Lake Lucerne Cruise.

Overnight: Same hotel (B, L, D)

Day 6: Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe + Interlaken

Eiger Express to Jungfraujoch. Ice Palace, Sphinx Deck. Visit Interlaken.

Overnight: Same hotel (B, L, D)

AUSTRIA Day 7: Rhine Falls + Swarovski + Innsbruck

Rhine Falls. Swarovski Crystal Museum. Innsbruck: Golden Roof, Maria Theresien Strasse.

Overnight: Alpenkoning / Alphotel or similar – Innsbruck/Seefeld (B, L, D)

VENICE Day 8: Venice + Gondola Ride

Private boat to St. Mark’s Square. St. Mark’s Basilica, Bridge of Sighs. Gondola ride.

Overnight: Four Points / Unaway / Capital or similar – Padova/Bologna (B, L, D)

FLORENCE & PISA Day 9: Florence + Leaning Tower

Guided walking tour: Duomo, Campanile, Piazza Della Signoria, Ponte Vecchio. Piazzale Michelangelo. Leaning Tower of Pisa.

Overnight: B&B / Park / A Point or similar – Tuscany (B, L, D)

ROME & VATICAN Day 10: Rome + Vatican City

Guided Rome tour. Vatican Museum, Sistine Chapel, St. Peter’s Basilica. Colosseum (outside), Trevi Fountain.

Overnight: SHG / Hampton / Marriott or similar – Rome (B, L, D)

Day 11: Fly Back Home

Transfer to airport. End of tour.
