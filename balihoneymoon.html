Detailed Itinerary

KUTA Day 1: Arrival Transfer

  • Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS)
  • Private transfer to Kuta Hotel
  • Check-in & relax
  • Overnight: Risata Bali Resort & Spa, Kuta

Day 2: Half Day Benoa Beach Tour

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Banana Boat Ride at Tanjung Benoa Beach (Private)
  • Enjoy water sports, shacks, and beach vibes
  • Free time to relax
  • Overnight: Risata Bali Resort & Spa, Kuta

Day 3: Full Day Tanah Lot & Uluwatu Temple Tour

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Tanah Lot Temple – Iconic sea temple on a rock
  • Late afternoon: Uluwatu Temple – Cliff-top sunset views
  • Witness dramatic Indian Ocean waves
  • Overnight: Risata Bali Resort & Spa, Kuta

UBUD Day 4: Kuta → Ubud

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Private transfer from Kuta to Ubud
  • Check-in at resort
  • Leisure time in Ubud
  • Overnight: The Lokha Ubud Resort, Ubud

Day 5: Full Day Kintamani Volcano & Ubud Village Tour

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Tohpati (Batik), Celuk (Silver), Mas (Woodcarving)
  • Mount Batur & Lake View from Kintamani
  • Experience Bali Swing
  • Coffee plantation visit & tasting
  • Overnight: The Lokha Ubud Resort, Ubud

Day 6: Departure Transfer

  • Breakfast & check-out
  • Private transfer to Ngurah Rai Airport
  • *** TOUR ENDS WITH MEMORIES OF PARADISE ***
